What is it ?

Linky is a website to share links between friends. Funny, amazing, revolting, or inspiring, share them all ! Just copy-paste a link, put a comment, and there you go, all your friends will see it.

How it works ?

Register, for free, to get an account. Once logged in, you will see the most popular links. Add friends, and you will see their links too. Posting a link is easy. Just copy-past your link, put a comment, and submit it. There, your link is know visible.
